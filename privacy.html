Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Poddy is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Local Data: Your podcast subscriptions, listening history, and preferences are stored locally on your device only.
  • Downloaded Content: Episode files you download are stored on your device.
  • No Account Required: We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • Contacts or other personal data

3. Third-Party Services

The App connects to third-party podcast feeds and the Podcast Index API to retrieve podcast information. These services may have their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. When you uninstall the App, all associated data is automatically removed. We do not maintain any cloud backups of your data.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security measures. We recommend using device-level security features such as passcodes and biometric authentication.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy with an updated date.

Contact Us
